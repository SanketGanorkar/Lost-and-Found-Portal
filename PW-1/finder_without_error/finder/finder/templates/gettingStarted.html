About Us

Welcome to Lost and Found Portal, your go-to platform for lost and found items on campus. Our mission is to simplify the process of retrieving lost belongings and reuniting them with their owners. At this portal, we leverage the latest technology to create a seamless and efficient lost and found experience. Our dedicated team is committed to ensuring a quick and secure return of items to their rightful owners.

Whether you've misplaced your keys, a valuable gadget, or any personal belongings, we've got you covered. Explore our platform to report lost items, track their status, and connect with the community to recover what matters most to you.

Thank you for choosing Lost and Found Portal. We're here to make sure your lost items find their way back to you!
